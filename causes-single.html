Safe Street Food

Street food plays an important socioeconomic role in meeting food and nutritional requirements of city consumers at affordable prices to the lower and middle income people. But, unfortunately, food handlers have been found to play prominent roles in the transmission of food-borne diseases and can pose a significant public health problem. Considering the problems, Padakhep initiated a sub-project entitled â€œPromotion of Safe Street Food Management Practices Project under the project of Sustainable Enterprise Project (SEP) since June 2021 jointly funded by PKSF and the World Bank. Padakhep is implementing the project in Mirpur and Mohammedpur area of Dhaka City Corporation and Rangpur City Corporation targeting 350 Micro-entrepreneurs who are engaged in Street Food Vending and Restaurant business. It aims to improve the overall business and environment of the microenterprises.

Major objectives of the project

  • Ensure safe street food for city dwellers
  • Reducing environmental pollution, occupational hazards and livelihood risks of street food vendors.
  • Provide technical know-how to ensure safety of street food.
